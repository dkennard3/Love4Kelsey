Who We Are

Service agencies for the developmentally disabled population are numerous; but through our own personal experiences, our leadership is keenly aware of the deficits that most of these agencies possess.

We are committed to gathering and implementing best practices from across the industry to make our service organization a trusted, quality agency that excels at meeting the needs of this very deserving population.



What We Do

Love For Kelsey provides in-home care to individuals with developmental disabilities. Services may include assistance with dressing, bathing, cooking, cleaning, companionship and/or just being in the homes for assistance as needed.
